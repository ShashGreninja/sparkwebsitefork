About Sparkonics

Sparkonics is the Electrical Engineering Society of IIT Patna.
Every society survives and grows through Skill development of its members, quality sharing of information and passing on the baton to the junior members who will handle the working of society one day.

Constructive culture started by founding members of a society are propagated forward by properly guiding young members and shaping their ideas. We, the members of Sparkonics act as a bridge to help juniors interact with seniors and get proper guidance which will help them in career alongwith keeping the work of society alive in future. The Student Mentorship Programme started by our founding members is being actively followed even today , where seniors guide juniors regarding how to know their area of interest and pick a project to work upon. These practices prepare young members to go in the right direction and also motivates them to take responsibility to handle Sparkonics in future to help their juniors.

Events

Static Rush

Static rush involves reaching various checkpoints with the help of clues they collect and complete the task if alloted. The rush stops after completing a final circuit.

Electroexquisite

Electroexquisite is the ultra exceptional quiz where you get to test your command over the electrical stream. This is also conducted in college fests.

Sparkfun

Sparkfun is an online story based treasure hunt type challenge, which dares the best of the best to complete it and win exciting prizes.

Ongoing Projects

Exoplanet Detection

 Artificial Intelligence, Deep Learning

This project titled "Exo-planet detection" is an attempt to create a model which predicts which bodies outside Solar System are planets and which aren't. Transit Photometry and techniques of Artificial Intelligence / Deep-learning are being implemented to make a working model with high accuracy.

Contact  

Safety devices for small fishing vessels

 AIS, Remote sensing, Microcontrollers

The objective of the project is to develop an effective fool proof mechanism that can forewarn host fishing vessels the presence of Ships and icebergs to make them safer during navigation at night by developing a device using a combination of sensors like Low Intensity Radar, AIS, GPS, etc.

Contact  

A non-invasive device for rapid screening of diabetic patients

 Sensor Technology, Internet of Things(IOT)

Current glucose monitoring methods for the ever-increasing number of diabetic people around the world are invasive, painful, time-consuming and a constant burden for the household budget. The non-invasive glucose monitoring technology overcomes these limitations.

Contact  

Autonomous Generation of Fiducial Coordinates

 Image Processing, Computer Vision

For any given DICOM (MRI/CT scan) series data of the patient with fiducials affixed, it is required to obtain autonomously the number of fiducials and coordinates of the centre points of the fiducials with respect any coordinate reference.

Contact  

Driverless Tractor

 Deep learning, Obstacle detection, GeoFencing & GPS, Web/App, IoT

This project includes designing tractors that independently observe their position, decide speed, and avoid obstacles such as people, animals, or objects in the field while performing their task and deliver a high tractive effort (or torque) at slow speeds for the purposes of tillage and other agricultural tasks.
